Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 19, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use Visual Search Pipeline, we may collect:

  • Uploaded images: Images you upload to perform visual searches. These are stored temporarily during processing and deleted automatically.
  • Search keywords: Keywords generated or entered during a search session.
  • Usage data: Basic usage logs (timestamps, search counts) for service improvement.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information solely to:

  • Process your image search requests
  • Return relevant search results
  • Improve the quality and performance of the Service

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage and Retention

Uploaded images are processed in memory and stored temporarily on our servers. All uploaded images are automatically deleted within 24 hours of upload. We do not retain search results or personal information beyond the active session.

4. Third-Party Services

Our Service interacts with third-party platforms (Pinterest, Instagram, TikTok, Google) to retrieve publicly available content. Your use of search results is subject to those platforms' respective privacy policies. We use Google's Gemini AI API to process search keywords — please refer to Google's privacy policy for details.

5. TikTok API Usage

We use the TikTok Research API to retrieve publicly available video data. We only access public video metadata (titles, URLs, thumbnails) and do not access private user data. Data retrieved via the TikTok API is used solely to display search results and is not stored permanently.

6. Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

7. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

8. Your Rights

You may request deletion of any data associated with your usage by contacting us. Since we do not retain personal data beyond active sessions, most data is automatically deleted.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at the email address listed on our website.
